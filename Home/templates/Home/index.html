What We Doing

Image Annotation and Data Labeling

We provide cost effective annotation services for machine learning, deep learning, logistics research labs and robotics companies along with artificial intelligence companies seeking high quality training data for diverse businesses by providing our clients customised image annotation services for various data analysis processes.

BOUNDING BOXES, POLYGON, & LINES

Datalab International has vast experience on annotating all shapes and sizes and to label and detect objects accurately while define lanes.Object detection, anomaly detection, predictive maintenance, yield estimations, and many other use cases across multiple industries

TEXT

Text classification and annotation for the implementation of natural language understanding and other text related machine learning models. Translation, entity extraction, text classification, summarisation and annotation across multiple industries and domains.

AUDIO

Speech to text transcriptions for the implementation of Natural Language Processing models. Speaker diarisation, call centre transcriptions, and various other use cases across multiple domains.

Data Capture & Validation Services

High quality Data Capture, Data Entry and Validation Services, capturing data from millions documents annually.
